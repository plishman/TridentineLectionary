It is truly meet and just, right and for 
our salvation, that we should at all 
times, and in all places, give thanks 
unto Thee, O holy Lord, Father 
almighty, everlasting God, through 
Christ our Lord. Who, after His 
Resurrection, appeared openly to all 
His disciples, and, while they looked 
on, was taken up into heaven, that He 
might grant unto us to be sharers in His
own divinity. And therefore with the 
Angels and Archangels, with the 
Thrones and Dominations, and with all 
the hosts of the heavenly army, we sing
the hymn of Thy glory, evermore 
saying:
