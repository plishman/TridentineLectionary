I am the root and stock of David, the 
bright and morning star. And the Spirit 
and the Bride say: Come. And he that 
heareth let him say: Come. And he 
that thirsteth let him come; and he that 
will, let him take of the water of life 
freely.

Alleluia are replaced by the Greater 
Alleluia)(In Eastertide the Gradual and Lesser
