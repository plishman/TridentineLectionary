It it truly meet and just, right and for our
salvation, that we should at all times, 
and in all places, give thanks unto 
Thee, O holy Lord, Father almighty, 
everlasting God; for when Thine only-
begotten Son was manifested in the 
substance of our mortal flesh, with the 
new light of His own Immortality He 
restored us. And therefore with Angels 
and Archangels, with Throne and 
Dominations, and with all the hosts of 
the heavenly army, we sing the hymn 
of Thy glory, evermore saying:
