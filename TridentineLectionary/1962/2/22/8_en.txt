We beseech Thee, O Lord that the 
prayer of the blessed Apostle Peter 
may commend the sacrifices and 
supplications of Thy Church, so that the
celebration we hold for his glory may 
profit for our pardon. 
for St. Paul)
Sanctify, O Lord, the offerings of Thy
people by the prayer of Thine Apostle
Paul, that those things which are 
pleasing to Thee by Thine own 
institution may become the more 
pleasing by the favor of his 
intercession.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, Thy 
Son, Who lives and reigns with Thee in 
the unity of the Holy Spirit, God, forever
and ever.
