It is truly meet and just, right and for 
our salvation, that we should at all 
times, and in all places, give thanks 
unto Thee, O holy Lord, Father 
almighty, everlasting God, through 
Christ our Lord. Who, ascending above
all the heavens and sitting at Thy right 
hand, poured out (on this day) the 
promised Holy Spirit upon the children 
of adoption. Wherefore the whole world
doth rejoice with overflowing joy and 
the heavenly Hosts also and the 
angelic Powers sing together the hymn 
of Thy glory, evermore saying:
